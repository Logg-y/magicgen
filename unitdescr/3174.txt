Nausicaa is a phaeacian princess and diplomat. She was the one who first encountered the Far Traveller, ship-wrecked and broken. She took an interest in his story and introduced him to her mother. When the Far Traveller left, Nausicaa developed a growing interest in foreign culture. With the blessing of her mother she left Phaeacia to discover the world. She has since visited many a court around the known world. Her beauty and fine manners have impressed may kings and she has become a most able diplomat and spy.
