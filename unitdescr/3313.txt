The cannibal tribes subjugated by Ind are controlled by virtuous men, but if left unchecked the heathens return to their old ways and follow their Shaman Chiefs as they did before they became subjects of the Magnificent Kingdom. As the Shaman Chiefs are few in numbers some tribesmen chose to follow self proclaimed warlords instead. These are not any better tacticians than the Shaman Chiefs and command even less respect. They are, however, equally prone to cause trouble.
