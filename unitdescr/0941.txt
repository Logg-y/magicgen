Celestial Masters are high Masters of the Way who have accepted title and training in the Celestial City. Skilled in magic and esoteric arts, they have been revered and respected as religious leaders since the birth of the Empire. Celestial Masters are versatile mages adept in many paths of magic, but first and foremost Water magic. As high Masters of the Way, Celestial Masters have perfected the art of internal alchemy and have gained longevity and the ability to fly.
