The Bouda Father is the first follower of Hyena. He is a werehyena of unequaled magical might. He usually appear as a witch doctor dressed in rags of hyena skin. As the first follower of Hyena the Bouda Father brought smithing to mankind as well as knowledge of death and diseases. Hyena is distrusted and feared, and the Bouda Father will cause some unrest in the province where he dwells.
