An Abbot Magus that has mastered the lore of the spheres is ordained as a Magus Supreme by the Prester King himself. A true master of the magic of the Spheres, the Abbot Magus Supreme would be known as the most powerful of mages, were it not that the Magnificent Kingdom and its marvels are unknown to all. The Abbot Magus Supreme have the privilege to regularly drink from the Fountain of Youth and most of them have lived for over a lifetime.
