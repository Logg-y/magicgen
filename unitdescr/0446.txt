Pazuzu is the Lord of the Plague Wind. He is a huge, feathery humanoid with four great wings, no head and the legs of a hen. Pazuzu is one of the Demon Lords. He will serve his summoner, knowing that the summoner will surrender his soul when he dies. Pazuzu is the bringer of storms and the Plague Wind of the desert, the dry wind from the east that brings disease and suffering.
