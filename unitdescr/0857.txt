The Smoking Mirror is a great Mictlan sorcerer who claims divinity. As master of magic, Lord of the Night Skies and the Sun Personified, he has become the supreme god of the Mictlan pantheon. He has the ability to disguise himself as a jaguar of exceptional size. With his smoking obsidian mirror, he can divine the fates of men. Like all gods of the Mictlan pantheon, he craves human sacrifices.
