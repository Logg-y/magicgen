When the Jarls of Jotun allied themselves with the humans and their Norns, the Gygjas of the Iron Wood rebelled and fought to regain lost influence, but their schemes were foreseen by the Norns, the rebellion was crushed and the Gygjas and their remaining allies withdrew to the Iron Wood. Now the remaining Jotun Gygjas guide the Vaettir as they once did their own kin. To the Vaettir the Jotun Gygjas are spiritual and religious guides as well as seers and political advisors.
