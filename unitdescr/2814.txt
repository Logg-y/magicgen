Since the first contacts with Therodos the mermen of Pelagia have been fascinated with the secrets of the Closed Realm, the Pelagian name for dry land. When Therodos sunk beneath the waves and the ancient kingdom became a haunt, the mermen turned their attentions to Berytos in search of secrets. With the aid of Berytos Pelagia founded colonies and coastal cities and soon sages and explorers emerged from the depths to search for the secrets of the Closed Realm.
