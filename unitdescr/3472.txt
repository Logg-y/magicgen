The Shayatin are a race of powerful Jinnun, spiritual beings born from Smokeless Flame. They serve the Sultans of Ubar with their silver tongues and crafty lies. As masters of manipulation they lead the enemies of Ubar astray. Shayatin are arrogant, proud and since magic has begun to wane they harbor a hatred for mankind, whom they blame for the loss of magic. It is they who convinced the Sultans to wage war upon humanity, they who doomed the City of Brass. Shayatin are masters of lies and can corrupt and lead the most loyal servant away from his master. Shayatin are pure-blooded Jinnun and share their traits, such as invisibility, glamour and a strong connection with magic.
