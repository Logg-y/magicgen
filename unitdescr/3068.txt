The Kusarikku are the fabled Bull Men of Enkidu legend and divine protectors of the plains of Ur. They have been revered by the Enkidus since before the founding of the First City, and are known to protect the Ensis and Entus as well as the entrances to the Great Temple. The Kusarikku are gifted with the strength of the earth and they wield spears that halt demons in their tracks.
