The Siddha is a divine being that has achieved physical and mental perfection. It is an inhabitant of the Celestial Sphere, although it originated in this world and rose to the heavens after enlightenment. The Siddha has four arms and is surrounded by an Aura of Splendor that strikes mortals with awe. Siddhas are powerful mages and priests. Siddhas are able to appear instantly wherever they want. They travel by Will and the Laws of Simultaneity.
