The onaqui is a being from the deep forests of Mictlan. It has a human torso, the legs and paws of a jaguar and the head and wings of a bat. It feeds on human hearts and its gruesome appetites cause unrest and discontent. Onaqui are powerful Blood mages and dark sorcerers. They serve the Bloodthirsty God as priests and mages. Onaqui are accompanied by a swarm of beast bats that grows with the Dominion of the Dark Lord.
