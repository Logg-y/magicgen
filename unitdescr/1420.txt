When the Atlantians fled into the deep swamps and jungles of Mictlan, they found a people that worshipped the Rain as one aspect of their God. The Atlantian kings became Kings of Rain and reshaped the faith to their needs. They found the secrets of the old blood cult and reinstated these foul practices of the ancient times. Now they gather power to wreak vengeance upon their old enemies and realize the dream of an eventual return to their former home beneath the waves.
