The Harem Guards, the Guardians of the Forbidden Chambers, are elite warriors guarding the inner chambers of the Ifrit Sultans' palaces. They are the personal bodyguards of the Sultans and their Houri concubines, and they rarely venture far from the blessed gardens of Jannah. Being entrusted with the personal safety of the Sultans and their concubines they are given enchanted armaments otherwise only used by the sacred Ifrit Warriors.
