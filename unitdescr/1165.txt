Some girls of exceptional bravery are trained in melee combat rather than archery. Shield Maidens fight with short sword and shield, weapons not used by the menfolk of Ulm. The use of shields was once despised as cowardly in Ulm, but since the Shield Maidens came out victorious from a number of battles in which they were outnumbered, they have become respected and are considered a force to be reckoned with.
