Sometimes a Raptor is born with black wings. This is a sure sign of greatness and the youngster is trained either as a Harab Seraph or a Raven Guard, the Raptor clan elites. The Raven Lord is a Raven Guard promoted to the rank of a commander. The Raptor Clan Caelians aren't cold resistant, so the Raven Guards don plate cuirass in battle. Raven Lords are stealthy.
