The Baobhan Sidhe is a Sidhe descendant of the Morrigans. She is a seductress and blood drinker who haunts the wilderness. She takes the appearance of a stunningly beautiful maiden and lures humans to her deadly embrace. Baobhan Sidhe are skilled in the use of illusions and Air magic. Baobhan Sidhe take great discomfort from iron and avoid it if they can.
