The tribe of Levi are known for their fanaticism and religious fervor. It is the only tribe that is allowed to train priests and sacred warriors. The Levite Zealots are driven by a fanatical loyalty to the lords of Gath and they often mete out justice where the hand of the Lord does not reach. The Zealots receive a bonus when patrolling provinces. They are sacred and can be blessed by priests. Their traditional colors are red, black and white.
