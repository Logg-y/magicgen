Satyrs are wild men with the hindquarters and horns of a goat. They are powerfully built and can easily outrun the swiftest of humans. Satyrs are selfish beings who lack discipline and will quickly flee from battle if the odds are against them. Their strong connection with Nature gives satyrs strong resistance to magic. This lighter satyr infantry uses shields and spears and sometimes carries javelins. All light satyrs are stealthy and useful as scouts.
