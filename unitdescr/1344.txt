The Devi is a demigod of immense size. She was once the wife of a previous Pantokrator. As a good wife and mother she was given power over the Great River and the fortunes of men. However, the Pantokrator lost interest in her and banished her to another world. Now with the Pantokrator gone she has returned to her land to claim it as hers. She is able to grant her followers the ability to breathe water and good fortune affects the province in which she dwells.
