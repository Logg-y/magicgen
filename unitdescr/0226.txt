The Arch Mage is a mage who has attained such power that he has mastered his own mortality. Donning a godly mantle, he has taken the role of a Pretender God. The Arch Mage is a master of magic and can be adept in several of the magic paths. The Arch Mage is a master of magic rituals and he can project his Elemental magic one province farther than normally possible.
